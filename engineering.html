intermediate

Haldia Institute of Technology is an institute of the Indian Centre for Advancement of Research and Education (ICARE) in Haldia, West Bengal. Also, it is the first private institute to be accredited with NAAC ‘A’ Grade and approved by AICTE. The NIRF 2021 rankings have ranked the HIT BTech program among the rank band of 201-250. A broad range of disciplines is taught at Haldia Institute of Technology to the students such as Engineering, Technology, Management, Basic Sciences, Applied Sciences, and Social Sciences.
